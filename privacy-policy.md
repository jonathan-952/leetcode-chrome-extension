Privacy Policy — LeetCode Recall

Last updated: March 6, 2025

LeetCode Recall ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension designed to help users track and recall LeetCode problems.

Data Collected
  When you use the Extension, we may collect:

  Email address
  Username
  Password (stored securely)
  User activity (e.g. problem submissions, solve history)

How We Use Your Data
  Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Extension's functionality, including saving your progress and personalizing your recall experience. I do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage
  Your data is stored securely. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.

Data Deletion
  To request deletion of your data, you can contact me at leetcoderecall@gmail.com.

Contact
  Questions? Reach out at leetcoderecall@gmail.com
